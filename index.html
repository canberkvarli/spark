Welcome to Spark

Here you can free your mind and let your-self flow into the unknown world of music!

  • With Spark, you can interact with an oscilliator by pressing your keyboard keys which will activate a note! (e.g. press 'Z' to play C note)
  • Click on the pads to play drum loops. For this purpose of the demonstration, they are already set to 120 bpm.
